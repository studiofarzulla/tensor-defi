## Key Results Summary

**Tensor Decomposition Performance:**
- Rank-4 CP: 96.55% explained variance
- Rank-4 Tucker: 96.56% explained variance
- Traditional PCA: 92.31% explained variance
- **Improvement: +4.24 percentage points over matrix methods**

**Four Interpretable Factors:**
1. Market-Wide Trend (synchronized movements)
2. Asset-Specific Dynamics (BTC vs ETH vs SOL)
3. Volatility Regime (high/low volatility states)
4. Microstructure Noise (venue effects, spreads)

**Data:**
- Exchange: Binance
- Assets: BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT, SOL/USDT
- Period: December 2023 - December 2024 (1 year)
- Frequency: Hourly OHLCV (8,761 observations)
